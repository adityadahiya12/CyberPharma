Pre-Test Precautions

For accurate test results, please follow these guidelines:

Fasting Requirements

For blood glucose and lipid tests, fast for 8-12 hours. Drink only water during this period.

Medication Disclosure

Inform your technician about all medications and supplements you're currently taking.

Hygiene Protocols

Maintain proper hand hygiene before sample collection to prevent contamination.

Hydration

Drink adequate water before urine tests unless instructed otherwise by your doctor.

Avoid Strenuous Activity

Refrain from heavy exercise 24 hours before certain tests as it can affect results.

Timing Matters

Some tests require specific timing (like hormonal tests) - follow your doctor's advice.
